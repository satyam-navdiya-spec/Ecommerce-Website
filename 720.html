Website Proposal: Digital Collage Portfolio

Prepared for: Dhruvi Devmurari

Project Overview

This proposal outlines the development of a professional, visually immersive website dedicated to showcasing a collection of digital collages. The site will serve as a digital gallery, focusing on high-quality visuals, fluid transitions, and a minimalist design to ensure the artwork remains the primary focus.

Key Features

  • Dynamic Gallery: A masonry-style layout to showcase various collage sizes and orientations.
  • Interactive Lightbox: Clickable images that expand to full-screen mode for detailed viewing.
  • Categorization: Filters to sort work by theme, year, or technique (e.g., surrealism, vintage, abstract).
  • About & Contact: A dedicated space to share your artistic vision and allow interested parties to get in touch.
  • Responsive Design: Fully optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile viewing.

Design Aesthetic

The design will utilize a clean, monochromatic color palette (whites, grays, and deep blacks) to ensure that the colors within the collages pop. Navigation will be kept intuitive to maintain a "gallery-like" experience rather than a traditional cluttered website.

Technical Stack

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.
  • Performance: Image optimization techniques to ensure fast load times without losing quality.
  • Hosting: Suggestions include GitHub Pages, Vercel, or Netlify for professional, free hosting solutions.
