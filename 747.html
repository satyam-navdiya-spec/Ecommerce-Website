College Website Development Proposal

Prepared by: Hardik Panaliya

Date: July 11, 2026

1. Project Objective

The goal of this project is to design, develop, and launch a modern, fully responsive, and accessible website for the college. The new website will serve as a digital hub to attract prospective students, keep current students informed, and provide a streamlined portal for faculty and alumni.

2. Scope of Work

The proposed website will include the following key features and pages:

  • Dynamic Homepage: Announcements, upcoming events, and quick links.
  • Admissions Portal: Application guidelines, fee structures, and downloadable forms.
  • Academics Section: Department directories, course catalogs, and syllabus downloads.
  • Student Life: Information on campus facilities, clubs, and hostels.
  • Faculty Directory: Profiles and contact information for professors and staff.
  • Responsive Design: Optimized for mobile phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Content Management System (CMS): An easy-to-use backend (e.g., WordPress) allowing college staff to update news and events without coding knowledge.

3. Project Timeline

The estimated completion time for this project is 8 weeks, divided into the following phases:

Phase Description Duration
Phase 1: Planning Requirement gathering, sitemap creation, and wireframing. Week 1-2
Phase 2: Design UI/UX design, mockups, and client approval. Week 3-4
Phase 3: Development Frontend and backend coding, CMS integration. Week 5-6
Phase 4: Testing & Launch Bug fixing, mobile responsiveness testing, and final deployment. Week 7-8
