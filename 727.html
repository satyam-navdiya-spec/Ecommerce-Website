College Website Development Proposal

Prepared and Presented by Jadav Parth

1. Introduction

In today's digital era, a college's website is its primary storefront, communication hub, and information center. This proposal, prepared by Jadav Parth, outlines a comprehensive plan to redesign and develop a modern, responsive, and user-centric website for the institution. The new platform will enhance the college's digital presence, attract prospective students, and streamline information access for current students and faculty.

2. Project Objectives

  • Modern & Intuitive Design: Create a visually appealing interface that reflects the college's prestige and values.
  • Mobile Responsiveness: Ensure seamless navigation across all devices (smartphones, tablets, desktops).
  • User-Centric Navigation: Reorganize the site structure so prospective students, current students, parents, and faculty can easily find relevant information.
  • Accessibility: Adhere to WCAG standards to ensure the website is accessible to users with disabilities.
  • Robust CMS: Implement a Content Management System (e.g., WordPress) to allow college staff to easily update announcements and events.

3. Proposed Features

  • Dynamic Homepage: Featuring high-quality campus videos/images, latest news, upcoming events, and quick links.
  • Academics Hub: Detailed pages for departments, courses, syllabi, and faculty profiles.
  • Admissions Portal: Step-by-step application guide, fee structures, scholarship info, and an online inquiry form.
  • Student Life Section: Information on clubs, hostels, sports facilities, and campus events.
  • Alumni Network: A dedicated space for alumni registration, success stories, and networking events.
  • Secure Login Portals: Integration links to student and faculty ERP/LMS dashboards.

4. Project Timeline

The estimated time to complete this project is approximately 8-10 weeks, broken down into the following phases:

Phase Description Estimated Duration
Phase 1: Discovery & Planning Requirement gathering, sitemap creation, and wireframing. 2 Weeks
Phase 2: UI/UX Design Creating visual mockups, color schemes, and finalizing layouts. 2 Weeks
Phase 3: Development Front-end coding (HTML/CSS/JS) and back-end CMS integration. 3 Weeks
Phase 4: Content & Testing Uploading initial content, SEO optimization, and bug fixing. 1-2 Weeks
Phase 5: Launch & Training Deploying the website to the live server and training staff on the CMS. 1 Week
