1. Executive Summary

In today's digital-first educational landscape, a college website serves as the primary gateway for prospective students, an essential portal for current students, and a critical resource for faculty and alumni. The objective of this proposal is to design, develop, and deploy a modern, high-performance, responsive, and secure institutional website that aligns with contemporary technological standards and modern user experience (UX) paradigms.

Spearheaded by Astha Patel, this project aims to replace outdated web infrastructures with a robust content management framework, ensuring lightning-fast loading speeds, absolute mobile responsiveness, accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1), and an intuitive portal layout for varied user archetypes.

2. Core Objectives

  • Unified Digital Identity: Establish a premium, modern, cohesive online brand presence that reflects academic excellence.
  • Persona-Driven Navigation: Streamline information architecture tailored specifically to Prospective Students, Current Students, Faculty/Staff, and Alumni.
  • Administrative Autonomy: Implement a highly secure, user-friendly Content Management System (CMS) enabling department heads to update content without technical friction.
  • Optimized Performance & SEO: Build with modern optimization protocols to ensure sub-2 second load times and top-tier visibility on search engines.
